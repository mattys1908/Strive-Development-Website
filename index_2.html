About the Game

What does it mean to Strive?

Strive is an intense action game about persistence, growth, and the cost of pushing your limits. Set in a fractured world where strength is earned through struggle, every encounter tests not just your reflexes — but your resolve.

Face relentless enemies, master a deep combat system, and carve your own path through a world that fights back at every turn. Defeat is not the end — it's the beginning of something stronger.

Genres: Action · Roguelike · Combat · Indie

Core Pillars

Built on Three Truths

01
Uncompromising Combat

Every fight is a conversation between intention and consequence. Learn enemy rhythms, exploit windows, and chain devastating combos with a system that rewards mastery.

02
A World Worth Exploring

Fractured environments filled with hidden paths, cryptic lore, and encounters that shift depending on how you play. Nothing is presented on a plate.

03
Growth Through Failure

Death carries meaning. Each run leaves traces — knowledge, scars, and power. Strive isn't about being good enough. It's about becoming better.
