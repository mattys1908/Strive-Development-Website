ABOUT THE GAME

What does it mean
to STRIVE?

An intense action game about persistence, growth, and the cost of pushing your limits. Set in a fractured world where strength is earned through struggle.

Every encounter tests not just your reflexes — but your resolve. Defeat is not the end. It is the beginning of something stronger.
